About Me

Who is Marlo Maddox Jr??

My name is Marlo Maddox Jr. I am a 22-year-old computer science major at Lincoln University in Pennsylvania with an interest in gaming. At a young age, I was introduced to video games and while video games had a bad rep at the time, playing video games gave me a second world to live in, and in this second world, I realized I can do whatever I want without any consequences, unlike the real world. As I got older, video games and techonogly became more advanced and technology as a whole began to grow on people over time and throughout that time. Video games, social media and virtual reality(VR) began to slowly influence the lives we today and Some people say it's a negative thing but I see it as a chance to improve life. I enjoy finding ways to add technology and gamify real world problems big or small. I hope to one day help everyone.
